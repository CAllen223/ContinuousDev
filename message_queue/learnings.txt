The main benefits in my mind of the topic based response is that you can control who can get your messages better, 
like sending one that only some of your contacts are meant to see, and to avoid the work that would come along with it.Aside from this 
you have the rabbitmw interface where you can test your connections before you implement them and bind queues to the exchanges there, as 
well as monitor connections made from scripts.

My main learnings from this assignment was how to use the rabibtmq technology, and further understanding of how docker-compose works.
The hardest part was learning how to read and write to files in the docker container, as well as timing the services so that they don't 
try to access something that isn't ready yet. For this end I used a 3rd part solution the wait-for-it.sh
